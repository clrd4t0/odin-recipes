Put Chai Ko 缽仔糕

Put Chai Ko 缽仔糕. Image Source

Put Chai Ko, or 缽仔糕 in Cantonese, is a steamed rice cup cake with red beans and it's one of the most popular dessert in Hong Kong. This recipe, courtesy of Christine's Recipes, will guide you to make a delicious Put Chai Ko that will surely bring a piece of Hong Kong with you everywhere you go!

Ingredients

To follow along, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Rice Flour - 85g
  • Wheat Starch - 55g
  • Brown Sugar - 110g
  • Water - 2 Cups
  • Red Bean Paste (Canned or Homemade) - To Taste
You will also need bowls with a capacity of 100ml or mini muffin trays to shape the cakes.

Steps

  1. Add rice flour and wheat starch into 1/2 cup of water and stir well.
  2. Use 1 and 1/2 of water to dissolve the sugar over medium heat. Bring to a boil. Quickly pour the result into the flour mixture. Keep stirring while pouring until well mixed. Add the beans into the mixture.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a mini muffin tray (or any other moulds) to steam over high heat for about 10 minutes. Add red beans during the steaming process on top of the cake to taste. If you are using bowls to steam, steam for 20 minutes.
